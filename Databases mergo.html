Databases

Mergosoft offers purpose-built databases that deliver high availability, enhanced security and scalable performance. Choose from a variety of database engines including relational and NoSQL databases: document, key-value, in-memory, graph, time series and ledger. Support for diverse data models makes it possible to build modern, highly scalable and distributed applications.

No one size fits all. By picking the most appropriate database for the job, you can better speed development and meet today’s business needs. DBaaS solutions on Cloud include auto provisioning, hosting, and 24×7 management with automated high availability, backup and restore, version upgrades, security and more.
